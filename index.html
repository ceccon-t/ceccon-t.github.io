Software Developer

I am a software developer and Scrum Master from Porto Alegre, Brazil. I currently work at SAP, where I help develop the technology to enable language services across the company. My background is heavily geared towards cloud-based systems with a focus on backend, but in my free time I also enjoy writing simple games and learning new languages and frameworks. Aside from that, I am very interested in how technology can help to shape society by enabling new possibilities and in how it interacts with culture and people's habits in general. Regular Automata

An educational tool developed as the final project for a college class on formal languages and automata. It allows the user to load a finite automaton (deterministic, non-deterministic or with empty movements) and run words to check if it accepts or rejects them. It's a typical Maven project, using Swing for the GUI.
Java

XP Diagnosis

A game used to assess a team's understanding and adoption of Agile values and eXtreme Programming practices. Team members pick random cards from the decks and discuss the topic that is described in that card and how it relates to their daily routine. Can be facilitated by a Scrum Master, Agile Coach, or someone else with deep knowledge of Agile and XP, who can explain the topics and answer questions as they appear.
